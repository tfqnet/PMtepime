ABOUT

PMtepi.me is a simplified portal for your profiles. Having too many social media profiles will get your contact confused, but by having PMtepi.me, getting in touch with you will never be easier.

WHAT

A combination of "Private Message" and "tepi" which means 'side' is a local slang for "message me privately". A perfect combination to represent our portal in which our main purpose is to help you organize all your social media websites and also your personal contact information. With just as simple as PMtepi.me/yourname, you shall have your personal digital business card in one single page. You may add your facebook, your twitter, your instagram and even your phone number or address. Anything that you want to include as your business card, PMtepi.me will get you there.
